## Maize disease classification model

This app uses the weights from [eligapris/maize-diseases-detection](https://huggingface.co/eligapris/maize-diseases-detection), published under the MIT License. The original TensorFlow SavedModel was converted without retraining to ONNX opset 17 for private in-browser inference. Its output order is Healthy, Gray Leaf Spot, Blight, and Common Rust.

The small demo image is also sourced from that model repository.
